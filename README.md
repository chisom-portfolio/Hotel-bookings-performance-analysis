# Hotel-bookings-performance-analysis

## Tools used
- Microsoft Excel

## Project overview
- The primary goal of this project was to analyze hotel bookings and data to identify high-value customer segments.
- By cleaning this raw data and letting go of redundant colums and performing a targeted analysis, it aimed at providing management with a data-backed strategy to maximize revenue and improve guest loyalty.

## Data cleaning and preparation
To ensure professional and acurate results, i performed the following:
- Removal of "Operational noise": Elimminated some redundanct colums
- Data privacy: Personal guest informations was removed to align with standard data protection(GDPR) practices.
- Feature engineering: Developed new calculation columns using formulas, specifically Total children and Total night spent.

## What i learnt
- Data privacy and ethicsL: I learned how to implement GDPR-aligned practices by identifying and removing sensitive PII(Personal Identifiable Information) like credit card numbers and email addresses.
- Feature Engineering for business logic: I praciced creating new variables, such as Total children and Total nights spent, to transform raw data into columns that actually tell a story about guest behaviour.
- Segment prioritization: I learned that while the hotel has many types of guests, the Transient segment is primary engine for profit. By doing this i was able to distinguish between "noise" and "value".
- Seasonality Analysis: By idntifying Auguest as the peak revenue month, I leaarned how to use data to pinpoint high-demand periods for optimized pricing strategy.

## Key Business insights
- Revenue drivers: The hotel generated between $26.8M and $29M in total revenue.
- Top segments: Identified the Transient customer type as the most profitable segment.
- Geography: Portugal(PRT) emerged as thb primary source market, followed by the UK.
- Seasonality: August was pinpointed as the peak earning month.

## Recommendation
- Target high-value segment: Focus marketing and services efforts on Transient guests to maintain the high shares of revenue.
- Strategic growth: By priortizing these valuable customer segments, the hotel can ensure consistent growth and make smarter, data-driven decisions.
